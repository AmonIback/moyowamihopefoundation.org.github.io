Our Works

Physiological & Psychosocial Counseling

Our team of specialized professionals are committed to bringing about positive changes in the mental and emotional well being of women and children under our programme. we offer an environment in which people are able to express their feelings and a gain a deeper insight into their difficulty with the aim of leading a healthier and happier life.

Maternal Health Care

To provide a holistic transformation of the maternal health Care system and one of our core aim is to provide skilled training to traditional birth attendants in rural settings. we also work hand in hand with Frontline health institutions and government to ensure their and our commitment of quality and efficient health care is fulfilled

Sexual Abuse Awareness

It is our core belief that child sexual Abuse is preventable. our team is hands on improving and increasing surveillance system and data collection for monitoring child sexual Abuse cases. we aim to increase understanding of risk and protective factors for child abuse perpetration and victimisation.

Legal Representation

We are committed to promoting justice and equity to those who need it the most. we highlight system failures and challenge the historical structures and hierarchies that discriminate against women and children. we believe that every woman and child that has suffered an injustice deserves every opportunity to have their day in court to be heard, believed and obtain a peaceful resolution.

Data Collection

Our team is established in various key places to gather and measure information in a systematic way with a mission of identifying key drivers of maternal mortality and child sexual abuse. All information is effectively screened in an effort to curb both vices.

Policy Programming

Our team works tirelessly to ensure programme consistency, credibility and accomplishment. it is our goal to ensure that all policies and programmes are achieved as aligned with our mission statement within the stated time period.

About Moyo Wami Hope Foundation

Organisation Summary

Moyo Wami Hope Foundation is an organisation founded on 25th of June, 2021 by Naomi Chipeta Chinguawa a Zambian based in United Kingdom and her team. The Foundation’s head office is located in Woodlands extension off Lunyema Road in Lusaka District of the Republic of Zambia and hopes to create branch offices across Zambia for the implementation of its activities and the achievement of its goals.

It is a non-political and non-profit making organisation that seeks to empower pregnant women with knowledge and rights during their journey of pregnancy in order to reduce maternal mortality, and ascertain that the ethical and legal challenges are acted upon to guarantee sustainability and effectiveness of the health care system in Zambia.

Moyo Wami Hope Foundation also seeks to enforce children rights more particularly children who have been sexually abused by providing both psychological and legal support and thereby providing a safe and conducive environment for the future of our children.

Our dedicated team member’s work together to investigate child sexual abuse as well as maternal neglect and work well to coordinate needed services.

Our Vision and Mission

OUR VISION

To have communities that have improved maternal health care for women that will lead to improved health indicators as well as communities that are safe and stable for our children.

OUR MISSION

We are committed to ensuring the prevention of maternal deaths as well as child sexual abuse so as to restore hope for expectant mothers and children affected by sexual abuse.

Main Objective

To provide a holistic transformation of maternal health care as well as prevention of child sexual abuse cases by offering access to a strong health system, providing knowledge and training to traditional birth attendants in rural areas, efficient and ethical professional care, strengthening of our Government’s commitment and capacity to fulfill children’s rights to protection so as to ensure children develop to their full potential. We at Moyo Wami Hope Foundation believe that every expectant mother requires special protection to prevent harm to their health and their infant’s health, it is also our firm belief that children who are key members of the human family are raised in safe and well nurtured environments. We are committed to ensuring our objectives listed below are fulfilled; -To enhance the maternal health care system by providing basic maternity care to all women as well as improve access to care of obstetric emergencies. -To support community-led initiatives with focus on using sustainable technologies to enhance development in maternal health care and prevention of child sexual abuse cases.

  • To ensure that children are safe, healthy and protected at home, in schools and communities by educating the public on one of the main rights of children which is the right to protection.
  • To provide legal representation for maternal neglect as well as child sexual abuse cases
  • To ensure improved coverage and quality skilled attendance at birth both at professional and traditional levels.
  • To facilitate the rehabilitation of traumatized children through physiological counselling and provision of nurturing environments.
  • To support community-led initiatives with focus on using sustainable technologies to enhance development in maternal health care and prevention of child sexual abuse cases.
